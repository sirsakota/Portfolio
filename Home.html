About me

I am a 27 year old IT-student, with long-standing enthusiasm for computers.

Ever since my father brought home a computer that was twice the sice of my current one when I was hardly older than 7,
I've had an interest in computers.
It was a huge box of a desktop computer, that took more than forever to boot
but I found myself exploring everything from video games
to the very basic paint application for hours.

I have retained my interest in gaming and by extension, coding, after my father allowed me
to play my very first video game on that PC.
It was just a simple horse game but it started my love for games that has persisted through the years.
At some point I became interested in video game development, hoping to see
how the games were made.
And in my thirst for knowledge, I made myself familiar with browsers, the Windows OS and then
suddenly I was our family's go to free IT support.
And since I was doing it for free, why not make it a career for real, I thought.
I have been pursuing computer hobbies all the way through the years to studying it,
as I started my studies in IT in 2020 since then.

My goal is to further pursue my education and start my career in programming and development.
After I finish my degree in programming, I am hoping to advance to university of applied sciences
in the same field.
